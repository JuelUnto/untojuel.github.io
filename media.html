"Hello" (Live Action & Stop Motion)

Description

This video is a Storytelling & Narrative assignment that was created in semester two in our First Year. This video contains Live Action and Stop Motion Animation. It is about a very shy guy who sees this lady in the park and sits on the same bench as him. He's attracted to this young lady but does not have the courage and the confidence to talk to her so his inner-self tries to assist him into talking to her. It does its best to help him and hopefully confronts her.

I hope you enjoy it!
